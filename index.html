Why New York?

NYC, the city of Dreams

New York City is one of the most-visited cities in the world. New York has many names - Concrete Jungle, Big Apple, the City That Never Sleeps and many more. New York is the place where one can live the life of the dreams.

One of the city's greatest attributes is its diversity and dynamism that offers many opportunities. The ambition, the connectivity, the entrepreneurial spirit, the ecosystem of innovation and the booming tech industry makes NYC the largest city in the country. It is the center of culture, commerce and everything.

My favorites places in NYC

Times square

Times Square is a major commercial intersection, tourist destination, entertainment hub, and neighborhood in Midtown Manhattan, New York City, United States.

Address

Manhattan, NY 10036

What is interesting about it

It is the liveliest area in the city with its neon lights and billboards. You can find a great atmosphere with restaurants, bars, theatres, museums and many more.

Central Park

Central Park is an urban park between the Upper West Side and Upper East Side neighborhoods of Manhattan in New York City, United States. It is the fifth-largest park in the city.

Address

New York, NY

What I like about it

Central Park is full of attractions, from green meadows to sprawling waters, gardens and unique bridges, music and performance centers, educational facilities, and more.

Learn More

Brooklyn Bridge

The Brooklyn Bridge is a hybrid cable-stayed/suspension bridge in New York City, United States, spanning the East River between the boroughs of Manhattan and Brooklyn.

Address

New York, NY 10038

What I like about it

Walking across this iconic bridge is one of the best things you can do in NYC. It's fun, free, and one of the perfect way to enjoy breathtaking skyline and river views!
